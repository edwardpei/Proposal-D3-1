Chinese History: Rise And Fall

Tens of millions of people died of starvation during 1959 to 1961 in China, yet in less than 40 years China became the 2nd largest economy in the world. There are many ways to explain the success of China’s last 4 decades, one of which is through the perspective of history. My purpose is using Chinese history to show China’s incredible ability of rising from low points, and also to show China’s capability of falling from high points. Therefore explaining the success China is having and suggesting China to be vigilant.
